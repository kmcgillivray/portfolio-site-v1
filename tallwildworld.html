Tall Wild World

October 1 – November 30

The Attic

Beautiful sea creatures, buggish alphabet creatures, a mysterious pixel zoo and the tallest, wildest animals you've ever seen. Tall Wild World is an exhibit of illustrations and art for children of all ages. It will be featured at the Attic bookstore and coffee shop in Green Bay during October and November. Be tall, be wild.


The Attic

730 Bodart Street, Green Bay WI Map


Opening Reception Tea Party

Saturday, October 5, 3:00 p.m. – 4:30 p.m.

Join in on a wild tea party, learn about how the illustrations were made, draw your own alphabet bugs and learn the secrets of the mysterious pixel zoo. One pixel or two?


Green Bay Gallery Nite

Thursday, November 21, 5:00 p.m. – 8:00 p.m.

The Attic and Tall Wild World are included in a ten gallery tour of the Olde Main Street district in Downtown Green Bay. This free event encourages attendees to enjoy an evening with the artists and also check out many of the Olde Main Street shops, restaurants, and galleries. Learn More


Come to the tea party or Gallery Nite, or view the exhibit anytime during
October and November at the Attic.
